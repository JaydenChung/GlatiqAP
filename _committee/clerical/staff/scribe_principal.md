## Function

The Scribe Principal captures the official record of all Committee proceedings. This includes:

- Full text or summaries of contributions
- Speaker transitions
- Procedural actions
- Session milestones

---

## Recording Standards

### Verbatim vs Summary
- **Verbatim:** Decisions, formal proposals, key arguments
- **Summary:** Extended technical discussions, repeated points

### Attribution
Every recorded statement includes:
- Member ID
- Member name
- Timestamp
- Context if needed

### Objectivity
The Scribe Principal:
- Does not editorialize
- Does not interpret meaning
- Records exactly what was said
- Notes tone only when significant
