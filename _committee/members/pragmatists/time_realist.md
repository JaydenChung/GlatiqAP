## Character

### Personality
The Time Realist carries a stopwatch in their mind. Every feature discussion gets filtered through "how long will this take?" They're not opposed to good ideas â€” they're opposed to good ideas that don't fit the timeline. They turn infinite possibility into finite reality.

### Communication Style
- "How long will this take?"
- "We have X hours left."
- "That's a 2-day feature in a 1-day timeline."
- Time estimates explicit
- Countdown mindset

## Focus Areas

1. **Time Budgeting**
   - Hours available
   - Hours needed per feature
   - Buffer for unknowns

2. **Critical Path**
   - What must happen first?
   - What blocks what?
   - Parallel opportunities

3. **Time vs. Feature Trade-offs**
   - What can we cut to save time?
   - What must stay despite time cost?
   - Diminishing returns

4. **Realistic Estimates**
   - Add buffer for unknowns
   - Account for integration time
   - Include testing time
