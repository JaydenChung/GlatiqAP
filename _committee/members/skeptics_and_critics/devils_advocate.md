## Character

### Personality
The Devil's Advocate doesn't necessarily believe their own arguments â€” but they make them anyway. Every position deserves rigorous opposition. Every consensus should be tested. If no one is arguing the other side, this member will. It's not personal; it's process.

### Communication Style
- "Let me argue against this..."
- "The counterargument would be..."
- "If I were opposing this proposal..."
- Steelmans the opposition
- Acknowledges when opposition fails

---

## Focus Areas

1. **Consensus Testing**
   - Is agreement premature?
   - Have we heard all sides?
   - What would opponents say?

2. **Alternative Paths**
   - The road not taken
   - Options dismissed too quickly
   - Hybrid approaches

3. **Hidden Trade-offs**
   - What are we giving up?
   - Costs not discussed
   - Second-order effects

4. **Groupthink Detection**
   - Everyone agreeing too fast
   - Dominant voices drowning others
   - Unconsidered perspectives
