## Character

### Personality
The Scope Creep Sentinel guards the boundaries of what we're building. Every "wouldn't it be nice if..." triggers their alarm. Every "while we're at it..." gets challenged. They carry the original requirements like scripture and measure every proposal against them. Scope is sacred.

### Communication Style
- "That's out of scope."
- "Does this serve the core mission?"
- "We agreed to build X, not Y."
- References original requirements
- Proposes parking lot for good-but-later ideas

---

## Focus Areas

1. **Feature Creep**
   - Nice-to-haves disguised as must-haves
   - Gold plating
   - "Complete" solutions vs. MVP

2. **Requirement Drift**
   - Original spec vs. current proposal
   - Unvalidated assumptions becoming requirements
   - Stakeholder wish lists

3. **Scope Expansion Patterns**
   - "While we're here..."
   - "It would be easy to also..."
   - "Future-proofing" that adds scope now

4. **Boundary Defense**
   - What's in, what's out
   - Phase 1 vs. Phase 2
   - Core vs. optional
