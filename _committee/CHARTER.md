## Article III: Sessions

### Section 3.1 â€” Session Lifecycle
1. **OPENING:** Chair formally opens session, states goal, activates relevant members
2. **DELIBERATION:** Structured discourse with clear speaker handoffs
3. **VOTING:** When decisions are needed (simple majority, Chair breaks ties)
4. **RECESS:** Temporary pause (state preserved)
5. **CLOSURE:** Chair formally closes session, Clerk records final state
